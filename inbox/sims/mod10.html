📜 Capital HUD

Liquidation Preference

The right of preferred shareholders to be paid a fixed amount (usually 1x original investment) before any payment is made to common shareholders.

Participation Rights

"Double Dipping." The right to receive the liquidation preference AND then share pro-rata in the remaining proceeds with common stock.
