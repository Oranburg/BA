The Attribution Problem

Can a decentralized protocol be a "legal person"? Without formal registration, the law often treats DAOs as unincorporated associations.

RUPA § 202 Default

If two or more persons associate for profit without a formal charter, they are a General Partnership, regardless of the technology used.
