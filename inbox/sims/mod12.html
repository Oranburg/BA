📜 Franchise HUD

SEC Rule 14a-8

Allows qualifying shareholders to include proposals in the company's proxy statement unless the company has a valid basis for **exclusion**.

Ordinary Business

Companies may exclude proposals that relate to the company's **ordinary business operations** (i.e., day-to-day management).
