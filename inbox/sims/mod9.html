Standard of Scrutiny: BJR

📜 Board HUD

Business Judgment Rule

A presumption that directors acted on an **informed basis**, in **good faith**, and in the **honest belief** that the action was in the best interests of the company.

DGCL § 144 (Cleansing)

No contract is voidable solely because of a director's interest IF: (1) Disclosed and Approved by disinterested directors, OR (2) Disclosed and Approved by shareholders, OR (3) Fair to the corporation.
