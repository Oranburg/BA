Unocal Standard

If the board adopts defensive measures, it must prove: (1) a threat to corporate policy, and (2) the defense was **proportionate**.

Revlon Duties

When a sale or break-up is inevitable, the board's duty shifts from preserving the entity to **maximizing shareholder value** (price).
